Only for college students

In order to register and log in to Campuslively, you must provide a valid .edu email address. This allows you to safely post your parties and events, knowing only other students will see.

Organized by school

Once registered and logged in, you can choose your school from a list of over 7,000. Once selected, you can view events and parties specific to your school.

See everything around you

Not only can you see what is happening on your campus, but you're also able to see all events near your school.

View events as a live feed

When viewing events and parties, you have two choices. The first is to view them as a live feed. This allows you to see new events and parties as they’re being posted, to see what other students are doing on those busier days and nights.

Or, view events as a calendar

The second choice to view events and parties is in calendar form. This is for when you want to see posts further into the future, allowing you to plan ahead and know what’s coming up.

Easily post your own events

As a student, you are immediately able to post your own events. When viewing events for your school, you will see the clear “Post Event” button. Want to get the word out about a party or event you’re organizing? Post it to Campuslively!
