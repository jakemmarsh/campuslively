Only for college students

In order to register and log in to Campuslively, you must be either a club or group, or provide a valid .edu email address. This allows you to safely post your parties and events, knowing only other students will see.

Organized by school

Once registered and logged in, you can choose from a list of available schools. Once selected, you can view events and parties specific to your school.

See everything around you

Not only can you see what is happening around your campus, but you're also able to see all events near you based on your location.

View events as a live feed

When viewing events and parties, you have two choices. The first is to view them as a live feed. This allows you to see new events and parties as they’re being posted, to see what other students are doing on those busier days and nights.

Or view events as a calendar

The second choice to view events and parties is in calendar form. This is for when you want to see posts further into the future, allowing you to plan ahead and know what’s coming up.

Easily post your own events

As a student, you are immediately able to post your own events. On any page, you will see the clear “Post Event” button. Want to get the word out about a party or event you’re organizing? Post it to Campuslively!

Easily post your events

Immediately after registering as a club/group, you will have the ability to post events. It's as simple as filling out one form and instantly reaching your target audience!

Connect to the students at your school

When you post an event, it becomes associated with the school you specified when registering. This allows student users to view your events when they're looking for something to do around campus.

Share with other users nearby

Your events can also be found by any user, regardless of school, if they are nearby the location you specified. This allows you to reach an even bigger audience!

Automatically reach your social media followers

In addition to the new users you'll reach, we also offer the ability to instantly connect with your followers from other social media sites. This means you can maintain your current fanbase while continuing to grow with Campuslively!

Interact directly with your target audience

Once you have registered as a Campuslively user, other users will be able to "subscribe" to you, allowing them to instantly get updates about your events. In addition, you can interact directly with your audience with quick comments and "like"s on the event details page!
